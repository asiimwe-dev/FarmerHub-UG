# Farmer Hub UG: Project Overview

## Strategic Repositioning

Farmer Hub UG has moved from a community-chat-first concept to a **B2B agri-intelligence infrastructure platform** serving cooperatives, processors, exporters, and agricultural lenders.

The product focus is now **FarmCom Core**: offline-first field intelligence, predictive yield aggregation, and trade-grade compliance traceability.

## Problem Being Solved

Uganda’s agriculture contributes strongly to GDP, but market coordination is still constrained by:

- fragmented smallholder supply data
- weak harvest volume predictability
- compliance pressure from global buyers (for example EUDR)
- limited digital operations in low-connectivity rural corridors

## Core Solution

Farmer Hub UG digitizes farm-level operational data through field agents and cooperative coordinators, then converts local records into:

1. verified aggregation intelligence for bulk buyers
2. pre-harvest volume forecasts for planning
3. geospatial traceability evidence for compliance workflows

## Primary Product Pillars

1. **Offline-first mobile data capture**
2. **Asynchronous sync and centralized aggregation**
3. **Geospatial boundary and provenance intelligence**
4. **Predictive yield modeling**
5. **Enterprise dashboards and compliance ledgers**

## Target Users

- Cooperatives and field operations teams
- Commodity processors and export buyers
- Microfinance and commodity lending institutions
- Compliance and trade verification stakeholders
