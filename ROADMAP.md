# Farmer Hub UG Roadmap (2026–2031)

## Horizon Goal

Build the leading East African offline-first agri-intelligence and traceability platform for cooperative aggregation and export compliance.

## Phase Plan

| Phase | Focus | Intended Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| 2026 | Product foundation and pilot setup | Stable field capture, sync gateway, first cooperative pilots |
| 2027 | Traceability and geospatial hardening | Production boundary mapping and verifiable batch provenance |
| 2028 | Forecasting commercialization | Enterprise yield intelligence subscriptions launched |
| 2029 | Corridor expansion | Multi-cooperative scale across target export corridors |
| 2030–2031 | Platform maturity | Regional data network with strong compliance and analytics offerings |

## Delivery Tracks

- **Track 6:** Agriculture and agribusiness management enablement
- **Track 12:** Monitoring and evaluation systems for operational intelligence

## KPI Themes

- Cooperative onboarding velocity
- Forecast confidence and stability
- Sync reliability in low-connectivity contexts
- Compliance ledger issuance volume
- Enterprise subscription retention
