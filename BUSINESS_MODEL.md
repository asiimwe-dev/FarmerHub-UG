# Farmer Hub UG: Business Model

## Revenue Design

| Revenue Vector | Operational Model | Customer Segment |
|---|---|---|
| B2B Clearing Commission | Percentage fee on contracts cleared through the platform | Agro-processors and global exporters |
| Data Intelligence SaaS | Subscription tiers for yield and supply risk intelligence | Lenders, processors, enterprise buyers |
| Traceability Verification | Flat fee per compliance batch ledger | Coffee, cocoa, and timber export chains |

## Market Size Assumptions

- **TAM:** $2.5B (global agri-traceability and intelligence software)
- **SAM:** $250M (East Africa specialty export corridors)
- **SOM target (3 years):** $4.5M

## Unit Economics (Planning Baseline)

- Enterprise subscriptions: **$5k–$25k/year**
- Marketplace commission: **3%–7%**
- SaaS/data gross margin: **70%–85%**
- Cooperative onboarding CAC: **~$2k**
- Estimated 5-year LTV: **~$40k**

## Financial Projection (3-Year Summary)

- **Year 1 revenue:** $0.6M
- **Year 2 revenue:** $2.0M
- **Year 3 revenue:** $6.0M
- **Target Year 3 EBITDA margin:** ~40%

## Go-to-Market

- Exporter/cooperative partnerships
- NGO and development agency deployment channels
- Subsidized donor-supported pilots
- Direct enterprise sales for commodity buyers

## Seed Use of Funds (Example)

| Use | Amount (USD) |
|---|---|
| Product and mobile operations | $700,000 |
| Partnerships and pilots | $350,000 |
| GTM and scaling | $300,000 |

## Key Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Cooperative adoption inertia | Field-agent programs and local enablement |
| Device access constraints | Subsidized device bundles |
| Slow early deployment velocity | NGO and donor pilot partnerships |
