## 📌 Roadmap Overview

### Strategic Vision
To move beyond passive data capture and become an active **Agri-RegTech** engine that automates global trade compliance and risk management.

| Horizon | Target Market | Focus | Revenue Target |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Phase 1** | Uganda | Coffee/Cocoa EUDR; TAP DWH Integration. | $4.5M (SOM) |
| **Phase 2** | East Africa | Regional EAC expansion (TZ, RW, ET, KE). | $250M (SAM) |
| **Phase 3** | Africa Hubs | West Africa Cocoa (Ghana, Ivory Coast). | Continental Play |
| **Phase 4** | Global | SE Asia (Palm Oil), Latin Am (Fine Flavor Cocoa). | $2.5B (TAM) |

---

## 🚀 Phase 1: Success in Uganda (2026)
**Goal**: Establish operational dominance and clear the EUDR Dec 30, 2026 deadline for large operators.

*   **Q1-Q2**: Launch TAP API integration with National Coffee DWH (NITA-U).
*   **Q2**: Deploy **Canopy-Decoupling Algorithm** $\Psi(k, \sigma)$ for Coffee-Banana systems.
*   **Q3**: Launch **Mediated Capture** training for 100+ field agents in Central & Mt. Elgon regions.
*   **Q4**: Generate first automated DDS (Due Diligence Statements) for EU export lots.

---

## 🌍 Phase 2: Regional East African Expansion (2027)
**Goal**: Scale the model across the EAC market.

*   **Tanzania**: Integrate with TZ legal frameworks for agricultural traceability in the Southern Highlands.
*   **Rwanda/Ethiopia**: Partner with UNDP programs to support local coffee cooperatives.
*   **Kenya**: Deploy high-resolution mapping for complex intercropped smallholder plots.

---

## 🔗 Phase 3: Continental Africa Scale (2028)
**Goal**: Transition to a continent-wide infrastructure provider.

*   **West Africa Cocoa**: Monitor agroforestry systems (AFS) in Ghana and Côte d'Ivoire.
*   **Risk Analytics**: Provide weather-risk and digital traceability for shea butter and cotton in Mali/Burkina Faso.

---

## 🌐 Phase 4: Global Market Penetration (2029+)
**Goal**: Capture share of the $2.5B global traceability software market.

*   **Southeast Asia**: Direct competition with SE Asian Ag-Tech for palm oil and rubber compliance.
*   **Latin America**: Advanced ML yield models (Seq2Seq-LSTM) for large-scale shaded coffee in Brazil and Colombia.
